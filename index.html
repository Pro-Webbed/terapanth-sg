Terapanth Background

Terapanth is a Jain Svetambara sect founded by Acharya Bhikshu (Swami Bhikanji Maharaj) in 1760 AD. The sect is currently led by its 11th Acharya, Acharya Mahashraman.

Terapanth emphasizes the nine core principles - non-violence (ahimsa), truth (satya), non-stealing (asteya), celibacy (brahmacharya), non-possession (aparigraha), tolerance (sahishnuta), equanimity (samata), service (seva), and self-restraint (sanyam).

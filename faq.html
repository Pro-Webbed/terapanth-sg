Frequently Asked Questions

What is the membership fee to join the Terapanth group?

The annual membership fee is $50 per family. This helps us organize events and activities throughout the year. For senior citizens (above 65), the fee is $30. Students can join for $20 per year.

How can I become a member of the Terapanth group?

You can become a member by filling out the membership form available on our Contact page and paying the annual fee. Once your application is processed, you will receive a confirmation email and be added to our WhatsApp group.

Who are the current members of the Terapanth group?

Our group consists of approximately 100 families across Singapore who follow Terapanth Jainism. The full directory of members can be accessed through the Community Directory on our Contact page (accessible to members only).

How often does the group meet?

We have regular monthly meetings on the first Sunday of every month. Additionally, we organize special gatherings for festivals and when Shamnijis visit Singapore.

Can non-Jains attend the events?

Yes, our events are open to anyone interested in learning about Jainism and Terapanth philosophy. We welcome guests and encourage them to participate in our activities.

How can I arrange for Shamnijis to visit my home during their stay in Singapore?

When Shamnijis visit Singapore, we create a schedule for home visits and gochri. You can book a slot through the link provided on our Contact page or by contacting our event coordinator directly.

What activities do you organize for children?

We have a dedicated youth program that includes Jain philosophy classes, cultural activities, and values-based workshops. These are typically held monthly and during special events.

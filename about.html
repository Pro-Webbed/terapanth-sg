Terapanth Jain Group Singapore

Who We Are

We are the Terapanth subsection of the Singapore Jain Religious Society (SJRS). Our community is dedicated to following and promoting the teachings of Terapanth Jainism in Singapore.

Established in 1995, our community has grown to include over 100 families across Singapore, and we welcome all who are interested in learning about and practicing Jain principles.

What We Do

We organize regular gatherings, religious ceremonies, and educational programs to promote Jain values and teachings. Our activities include:

  • Monthly gatherings for jaaps, prayers, and discussions
  • Special celebrations for Paryushan and other festivals
  • Hosting Shamnijis during their visits to Singapore
  • Educational programs for children and adults
  • Community service and charity events

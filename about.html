Terapanth Jain Group Singapore

We are the Terapanth subsection of the Singapore Jain Religious Society (SJRS). We frequently gather at least once a month for either a special occasion or to come together for Jaaps or performances. Every year, during Paryushan, we invite the Shamnijis for gochri or to stay at our house for the night. So if you want to get a slot, you can come to this website.
